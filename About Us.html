What is Manchester United About?

Manchester United is one of the largest football clubs in the world. Over the years thye have changed the history of the sport and will always be remembered for there way playing the game. Manchester United are not your average football team as they have achieved greatness in all forms of football nationally and internationally.They also have one of the largest fan bases of any football club all over the world. Their large and supporting football community provides Manchester United with a worldwide platform to generate large amounts of revenue through many different sources such as sponsorships,merchandising, broadcasting and the normal selling match day tickets. By doing these Manchester United has become one of the most wealthy clubs in the world, this is due to its large success and huge fan base. They attract leading global companies such as adidas, Aon, and General Motors (Chevrolet) that want access and exposure to our community of followers and association with our brand.

Supporters and Followers

Manchester United has many supporters all accross the globe due to the international players they have in there squad. Statstics have shown that the club has over 659 million followers worldwide coming from all accross the globe. A large survery conducted has shown that the club has 71 million followers in America, 90 million in Europe, 173 million in Africa and the Middle East and over 325 million in Africa and the Middle East. So just by looking at these figures we can see how large the club is in terms of the followers and supporters they have all over the world. The largest sports survery ever conducted has named Manchester United the worlds most popular club based on the figures shown above and it is not hard to see why it has been given this title looking at the large amounts of support it has all over the world. This survey was conducted by leading market research agency who gathered the data from over 54,000 people from over 39 countries so they had a large data set to compare, after this survey was conducted Manchester United was also named the Most Valuable Club in the world. Source Kantar, May 2012.
